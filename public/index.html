How I Built This Bot

This is a regime-adaptive scalping bot — an automated trading system that adapts its strategy based on current market conditions. It monitors three assets simultaneously — BTC, ETH, and SOL — on 15-minute candles, 24/7. No guessing, no gut feelings, no emotions.

The bot detects the current market regime using ADX (Average Directional Index) and switches between two strategies:

  • Mean Reversion (ADX < 25, ranging market) — buys when price touches the lower Bollinger Band with oversold RSI, expecting a bounce back to the mean
  • Breakout (ADX > 35, trending market) — buys Donchian channel breakouts confirmed by volume and momentum, riding the trend
  • It also takes SHORT trades — selling when RSI is overbought above the upper Bollinger Band, or when price breaks below the Donchian low during downtrends

Each trade must pass multiple filters including volume confirmation, RSI range checks, and Bollinger Band positioning. If even one fails, it waits.

The Testing Process

I built a backtesting engine that replays the strategy against 30 days of real historical price data to see exactly how it would have performed. The strategy evolved through multiple iterations:

  • v1–v3 — Simple EMA crossover scalping on 1-minute candles. Progressively tighter filters improved win rate from 48% to 50%, but the minute-by-minute noise was problematic.
  • v4 — Upgraded to 5-minute timeframe with higher-timeframe trend filtering. Better signal quality.
  • v5 — Added SHORT support alongside LONGs. Both directions using RSI, Bollinger Bands, and Donchian channels.
  • v6 — Current version. Full regime-adaptive strategy on 15-minute candles across ETH, BTC, and SOL. Tighter risk management: 5% daily loss limit, 3-loss consecutive circuit breaker with 2-hour halt.

Safety Nets

The bot has multiple layers of protection:

  • Stop-loss — limits the maximum loss on any single trade (10% max position size)
  • Take-profit — locks in gains automatically based on the strategy
  • Breakeven trigger — once a trade moves sufficiently in the right direction, the stop-loss moves to the entry price
  • Daily loss limit — halts all trading if daily losses exceed 5%
  • Consecutive loss breaker — 3 losses in a row triggers a 2-hour cooldown
  • Kill switch — permanently stops if total drawdown exceeds 40%

Everything you see on this dashboard is happening in real time. The bot never sleeps, never panics, and never makes emotional decisions. It's currently running in paper trading mode — simulating trades without real money — while I validate the strategy in live market conditions.
