How This Bot Works

This is an automated trading bot that watches the price of Ethereum (ETH) — one of the largest cryptocurrencies — every single minute, 24 hours a day.

Instead of buying and hoping the price goes up, it uses a set of mathematical rules to decide exactly when to buy and when to sell. Think of it like a very patient fisherman who only casts the line when conditions are perfect — the right water temperature, the right time of day, the right bait.

The bot checks 8 different conditions before every trade:

  • Is the short-term trend crossing above the long-term trend? (like a car accelerating past another)
  • Is the momentum in the right range — not too hot, not too cold?
  • Is trading volume higher than normal? (more people trading = more confidence in the move)
  • Is the bigger picture also pointing up? (checking the 5-minute trend, not just the 1-minute)
  • Is it the right time of day? (we only trade during the two windows where this strategy performs best: morning London and late New York sessions)

All 8 conditions must be true at the same time before the bot will buy. If even one fails, it waits. This means it skips the vast majority of opportunities — but the ones it takes have a historically high success rate.

Once in a trade, the bot has automatic safety nets: a stop-loss that limits how much it can lose, a take-profit that locks in gains, and a breakeven trigger that protects profits once the trade moves in the right direction.

Everything you see on this dashboard is happening in real time. The bot never sleeps, never panics, and never makes emotional decisions.
